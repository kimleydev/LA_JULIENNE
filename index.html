About Us

Who WeAre

Catering

La Julienne was founded in 1970 by french immigrants Laroche and Laurelle Posay. They opened their bakery with three promises...

  • Authencity is the priority.
  • Freshness is GUARANTEED.
  • Vive La France!

    • As LaRoche and Laurelle Posay came to New York, they noticed the lack of authencity in the so called French restaurants. They knew they had to take matters into their own hands and opened up a little bakery right under their apartement and named it after LaRoche's grandmother: "La Julienne" From then on, There has never been a more authentic French bakery in NYC.

Our Goal

We hope that in our bakery you will find all your favorite treats, and maybe, try some new ones.
